Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy: they’re important, but no one likes to read them. We’ll try to keep this as understandable and succinct as possible.

Collected Information

We believe in privacy respecting software. The only personal information we collect is your email address. We will use this to provide you access to use Mythic and to communicate with you about your account, support, and changes to Mythic.

To do this, your email will be provided to Okta for the purpose of creating and managing your account and authentication credentials. Additionally, your email will be provided to MailChimp as our mailing list service provider. You can read their end-user privacy policy for how your email and data will be handled. (tl;dr - they don’t sell or provide your address to anyone else)

We will not sell or provide a third-party with your email address except as stated above.

Analytics

Hotjar

We use Hotjar to help understand how you and our members use Mythic. Hotjar is a technology service that helps us better understand our members' experience (e.g. how much time they spend on which pages, which links they choose to click, what they do and don’t like, etc.) and this enables us to build and maintain our service with member feedback. Hotjar uses cookies and other technologies to collect data on our members’ behavior and their devices. This includes a device's IP address (processed during your session and stored in a de-identified form), device screen size, device type (unique device identifiers), browser information, geographic location (country only), and the preferred language used to display our website. Hotjar stores this information on our behalf in a pseudonymized user profile. Hotjar is contractually forbidden to sell any of the data collected on our behalf.

For further details, please see the "about Hotjar" section of Hotjar’s support site.

Matomo

Along with Hotjar, our primary source of analytics is Matomo Analytics. Because we self-host the software, no data is sent to Matomo. We have disabled tracking cookies and will respect Do Not Track signals from your browser. When you visit Mythic, we will collect and store: the website from which you visited us, the parts of Mythic you visit, the date and duration of your visit, your truncated and anonymised IP address, and information from the device (device type, operating system, screen resolution, language, country you are located in, and web browser type) you used during your visit. We process this usage data in Matomo Analytics for statistical purposes, to improve Mythic and to recognize and stop any misuse.

Page URLs containing storybook, folder, and page titles are all anonymised before being processed meaning that none of your content is captured in the analytics software.

We do not sell or provide third-parties with our analytics data.
