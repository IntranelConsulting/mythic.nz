## Content Sharing

> This feature is under development.

Storybooks and (soon) individual pages on Mythic can be shared with other people on the platform or the general public. Eventually, these permissions will grow to include the ability of others to contribute to your content. Currently, read-only sharing of Storybooks is implemented. Here's how to use it.

### Storybook Sharing

There are two places you can share a Storybook from: the settings modal of a Storybook on your Dashboard, or the settings modal accessed through the top level menu when you have entered a Storybook.
